Rules

Entering a game

Every game has an entrance fee of 50$.

You cannot enter a game if your credit is less than 60$ (a minimum of 10$ is considered for the blind bet).

Blind round

You have to place the blind bet. Given that this poker game is based on Limit Texas Hold'Em, you have \n 3 options for the blind, the same as for raise: 10$, 50$ and 100$.

After you've placed the blind bet, the robots will start betting too.

You can inly fold after the blind round.

Flop round

The flop (the first 3 cards on the table) are now flipped.

Once it's your turn, you will start the second round of betting. As in classic poker, you can choose to fold, check or raise.

If you fold, the money that were previously bet will be lost.

Turn round

The turn (the fourth cards on the table) is now flipped.

Bets continue normally, as stated before.

River round

All the community cards are now visible. This is the last round of betting, after which the winner will be chosen.

All remaining players' hands are evaluated and the one with the highest rank wins.

The winner takes the pot.
